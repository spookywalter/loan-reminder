Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Loan Reminder helps users manage loans, due dates, and payment records. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as username, email, and encrypted password.
  • Profile details you provide, including optional phone and personal profile fields.
  • Loan and payment records entered by you while using the app.
  • Support messages or queries submitted through the platform.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the loan management service.
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • To send reminders, payment notifications, and support responses.
  • To improve reliability, safety, and feature quality.

3. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including secure transport (HTTPS), access controls, and password hashing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers required to run core platform functions, such as hosting, database, and notification services.

5. Your Choices

  • You may update profile information from your account settings.
  • You may request account and related data deletion by contacting support.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: support@loanreminder.com
